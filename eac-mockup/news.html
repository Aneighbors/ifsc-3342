The Success of The VEX Robotics Summer Camp

The Robotics camp has successfully wrapped. A huge thank you to all the participants, volunteers, and instructors who made the camp an unforgettable experience. Special thanks to KARK 4 and FOX16 for featuring our camp and the talent of the campers. All the support and dedication have truly made a difference.

More than 20 students in grades 5-8 took part in a robotics competition on June 14 to celebrate the end of the UA Little Rock VEX Robotics Camp. Led by Dr. Sandra Leiterman, managing director of the Cyber Arena, students gained valuable skills in robotics, programming, and teamwork by utilizing VEX IQ to design, construct, and program their robots.

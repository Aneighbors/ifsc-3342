Why this blog, April 4th, 2023

I decided to create this blog to help and inspire other women, wives and/or mothers who feel like they are struggling.

Passionate Penny Pincher

My Favorite Website, May 8th, 2023

One of my favorite websites that I took some inspirations from for my website is called Passionate Penny Pincher. Click here to go to their website. This website has recipes, DIY ideas, money saving tips and more. I receive emails from them about things that are on sale, ways to save money, tips, and tricks they have discovered, recipes, you name it. I hope you enjoy this website as much as I do! I have even purchased 3 of their recipe boxes and my family so far has loved all the recipes that we have cooked. I have also bought their planner for 2 years in a row now. Their planners are beautifully made, high quality and just like their website filled with useful information. I have also saved countless of dollars from following their coupon/sales, they even will share ways to get some things for free!

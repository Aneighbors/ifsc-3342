Why Date? 05/03/2023

So I am married, so why am I calling this section of my blog "dating" and not "marriage"? Well I am going to tell you teh secret to a happy marriage--dating! You cant ever stop dating your spouse. You need to always pursue them like you did when you were dating. My husband's name is Gage, he and I had got into a rut that most married couples seem to fall into- we were feeling more like roomates than spouses. But why? Because life had got in the way. When your young and dating you make time to see each other, you look forward to seeing each other, talking to each other. But when the stresses of everyday life gets in the way its easy to out your partner on the backburner. But this should never be the case. Your partner is your one and only. You need to make time for each other even if its just one night a month. Never stop dating. In my next blog post I am going to talk about two books that we have used to help make dating more spontaneous and fun.
